About me

Basically...

I am a dedicated IT enthusiast with basic-to-mid experience in web and software development. I have this need for trying to learn new skill especially in web-dev whenever I have a free time; hoping to make it as a full-time job eventually. My main job is currently working as Information Technology Officer at IIUM at IIUM; International Islamic University Malaysia doing all kind of related webapp project; specifically maintaining existing application and solving application issue. I learned a lot from those awesome people at work. I was nominated for the President Award at the end of my study in Universiti Kuala Lumpur – MIIT during 2017; an achievement that I thought was so out of reach. After my convocation, I started my journey as a blooming researcher in IT; gaining the experience and life’s lesson along the way. Now, I’ve started to formulate my goals and some achievable feats to attain as I progress through life. There will be bumps and stones and those prickling step-backs, but I believe with positive attitudes and strong work ethics; I can face them head strong.


Other things I do..

I do love to read, that is my niche; especially those that involves with dragons and logic-defying physics. I also manage to learn how to develop website in the most; possibly, easiest way possible in my free time; I’m looking at you 'frameworks'. What is more, drawing digital artworks has always been my favorite-things-to-do in past time; or at least when I’m bored and when sleep at that time is a no-no. I especially love Japanese cultures and the things they usually contribute to the world; yes I grew up with Dragonballs and Naruto. I love to teach awesome, practical things I’ve learned in my life to people who is willing to be taught. Teaching and helping people put some well-deserved steaming hot baked brownies in my plate of happiness.
